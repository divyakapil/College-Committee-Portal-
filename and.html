ISTE

RAIT has ISTE student Chapter and ISTE Faculty chapter.During an academic year the students chapter organizes technical event INNOVISION for odd semester and TECHNOZION for even semester.The main motto is Career Development of Teachers and Personality Development of Students.

ISTE


CSI

Computer Society of India (CSI) - RAIT is the student chapter of CSI. During an academic calendar they organise Techmate and Techknow: the tech fests and three annual publications: Cozine,Technophilia and Decrypt.

CSI


ISA

ISA - RAIT is the student chapter of Department of Instrumentation. During an academic calendar they organise Genesis and Niyantran-the tech fests and three annual publications-ISAAC magazine; Automatrix and Insight newsletters.

ISA


IEEE

IEEE - RAIT is the student chapter of IEEE. During an academic calendar they organise Whizion and 360 Degrees-the tech fests and three annual publications.

IEEE


Kalaraag

Kalaraag is a unique endeavour started by the students to show their talents in fine arts and creative art.It has consistently proved its mettle in performing art by winning accolades in folk dances,skits theatre and other forms of art by winning prizes for many years at the university level.So professionally has it presented itself that it has links in the Marathi theatre and cinema world.

Kalaraag

Social Wing

Social Wing was formed in September,2013 and has undertaken activities like teaching underprivileged children,awareness drives(through seminar/street plays), various environmental activities(clean up drives,promoting renewable energy resources etc). The Social Wing is open for everyone who wants to volunteer for many such activities.

Social Wing

Sports

Students of RAIT are not only known for their academic performance but are also sports enthusiast they play Cricket, Football, Basket Ball, Table Tennis,Swimming and various other sports.D.Y.Patil Sports Academy encourages the students to utilise all the resources available in the campus. RAIT organises Stamina which is a 3-day Intra RAIT sports tournament that sees top participation for all events.

Sports

ITSA

ITSA-RAIT is the student chapter of Department of Information Technology.Indian Telecommunication Services Association is an Association of ITS officers.The Indian Telecommunications Service,widely known as ITS,is an organised Group A Central Civil Service of Government of India.The appointment to this service is done through Combined Engineering Services exam held every year by UPSC of India.
